Privacy Policy

We keep it simple and straight-up. Here's how we handle your data.

1. Who We Are

WagerX ("we," "our," or "us") operates the WagerX website and services. We're an Australian company dedicated to helping punters make smarter betting decisions using AI-powered tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you subscribe, we collect your email address and payment details (processed securely through Stripe).
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our website, including pages visited and features used.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data like browser type, device type, and IP address for security and analytics.
  • Discord Data: When you join our community, we may collect your Discord username for verification purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you betting alerts and important updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Verify your access to our Discord community
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Stripe's security practices meet the highest industry standards (PCI-DSS Level 1).

5. Data Sharing

We don't sell your personal data. Ever. We only share data with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Discord: For community access verification
  • Analytics providers: Anonymized usage data to improve our service
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by Australian law

6. Data Security

We take security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmission and store data on secure, Australian-based servers where possible. We regularly review our security practices to keep your data safe.

7. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at info@wagerx.com.au.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep the website running smoothly and analytics cookies to understand how people use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (bookmakers, etc.). We're not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Have a read of their policies before sharing your data with them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any significant changes via email or through our Discord community.

11. Contact Us

Got questions about your privacy? We're happy to help.

  • Email: info@wagerx.com.au
  • Discord: discord.gg/wagerx
